"Explore The Temple Village"


Interested in visiting Andhra tourist places? Annavaram is a small town situated on banks of river Pampa which is 142 km away from Hyderabad. Tourist visit Annavaram as it is popular for Sri Veera Venkata Satyanarayana Swamy, who is a Hindu God. Thousands of devotees visit Annavaram for worshipping and performing special prayers.

Location: Annavaram
Best Time To Visit: Throughout the year
Famous for: Spiritual devotees
