Our Story

Website was founded in 2021 by our team who wanted to show people the side of a country that not everyone gets to see. Our team was lucky enough to travel the country with local friends who took us to places and shared things we would never have experienced this. We felt there was a space to create these unique travel experiences with a personal, human touch for others Though we started out just selling trips to Others now sells trips around the world; and despite our growth, Personal is how we began, and that is how we continue to be.
