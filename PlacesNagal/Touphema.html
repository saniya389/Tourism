TOUPHEMA VILLAGE

"Traditional And Heritage Experience"


On a green and verdant hillock, lies the township called Touphema Village. Built by the local community in partnership with the Tourism Department of Nagaland, Touphema Village is a collection of small huts built and decorated in traditional Naga design sensibilities. The village intends on giving the tourists the feel of living in a Naga tribal house. Touphema village situated about a hundred kilometres from Dimapur and is a wonderful tribute to the culture, lifestyle and the people of Nagaland. Living at the village teaches you so much about country life and Naga culture in particular that it becomes an experience in itself. It is a highly popular place to visit in Nagaland and deservedly so. Make sure you book in advance in order to enjoy the experience. In addition to the stay and food, the attractions include traditional Naga performances such as folk dances and the retelling of folk tales.

Location: Kohima.
Best Time To Visit: Throughout the year.
Famous for: Khonoma Fort, Angami Bridge.
