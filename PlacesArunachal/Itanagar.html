"True Local Culture"


When you are going around different places, how can you miss the capital city itself? Itanagar is a well-developed township that is 350m above the sea level. This city can be envied for the rich and cultural architectural wonders, making it one of the most interesting places to visit in Arunachal Pradesh. You can visit the Ita Fort which is made of special breaks. The legendary Gyakar Sinwi which is also called as Ganga Lake is another interesting spot. It is 7km away from the city. A museum which is the repository of the indigenous tribal culture of Arunachal Pradesh known as JawaharLal Nehru Museum is another wonder.

Location: Itanagar, the capital of Arunachal Pradesh. You can reach here from Guwahati airport, or from Tezpur airport too. You can also take luxury bus services from the nearest cities of Assam.
Best Time To Visit: Whole Year
Famous For: Cultural Architectural Wonders
