"Rich In Heritage"


Welcome to the land of orchids, tigers and some ancient historical ruins. One of the top places to see in Arunachal Pradesh if you are a history lover, you will find the remnants of King Bhaluka, a former ruler of the region. It is not a wonder that the small town is named after their ancient king. The Kameng river flows right through the town and provides amazing avenues for picnic and adventure tourism. The tourist attractions around the town are the Tipi Orchidarium, Bhalukpong Fort and the Pakke Tiger Reserve.


Location: West Kameng
Best Time To Visit: Throughout Year
Famous For: History lovers, sightseeing
