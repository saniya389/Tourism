KEM LIAT PRAH

"Natural Wonders"


Meghalaya is home to numerous natural wonders including an uncountable number of natural caves, some of which are still unexplored. If you are into caving or if you like to be intrigued by mysteries, Krem Liat Prah – the longest natural cave in India – deserves your visit. The current length of the cave is 34 km which is expected to grow as more connected caves are discovered. Located in Shnongrim Ridge of East Jaintia Hills District of Meghalaya, Krem Liat Prah is one of the 150 known caves in the region. Owing to its unbeatable fame among explorers, it easily finds its name among the top Meghalaya tourist places. Book an economic tour package of Meghalaya which includes all the major tourist attractions of Meghalaya like Krem Falls, Mwasmai Cave and few more.

Location: Shnongrim Ridge of the East Jaintia Hills.
Best Time To Visit: October to May.
Famous for: Krem Falls, Mwasmai Cave.
