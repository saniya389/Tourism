FATEHPUR

"Fatehpur, Rajasthan, Ancient City, Rich History, Shekhawati Region."


Situated in the Sikar district of Rajasthan, Fatehpur is an ancient town, a prominent part of the Shekhawati region. Fatehpur is popular with tourists who want to try out a new destination, and have a knack for art and culture. Easily reachable on the Delhi- Jaipur highway, Fatehpur has a number of palaces and forts adorning the beautiful craft of fresco. For a break in the monotonous routine, Rajasthani hospitality, love of art and culture, head to Fatehpur.

Location: Rajasthan
Best Time To Visit: February to March ,October to November .
Famous for: Rajasthani hospitality, love of art and culture.
