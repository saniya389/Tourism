ARVALEM WATERFALLS

Previous Next

"Also known as Harvalem Falls!!!"


One of the most beautiful spots to visit in North Goa, Arvalem Waterfalls is located in Sanquelim, about 30km away from from the Panjim Kadamba bus stand. Also known as Harvalem Falls, it forms a huge pool of water at its base - almost as big as a lake. This makes Arvalem Falls a popular tourist and picnic spot. The fall is best visited during the monsoon as after that, it reduces to just a trickle. GTDC has developed a park in the location with protective railings so that the visitors can get an even better view of the falls and also a good opportunity to take aesthetic pictures. The falls are also surrounded by natural beauty and Rudreshwar Temple.

Location: in Sanquelim, about 30km away from from the Panjim Kadamba bus stand,North Goa.
Best Time To Visit: After monsoon.
Famous For: natural beauty and Rudreshwar Temple.
