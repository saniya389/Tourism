"Oil City of Assam"


Popularly known as the Oil city of Assam, Digboi encompasses lush green tea gardens and is the focal point of the Tinsukia district’s economic activities. Famous for housing India’s oldest refinery, this town in Assam has a historic significance to it as Crude oil was discovered here in the 19th century. Apart from its significant historical episodes revolving around oil production, Digboi is fascinating and carries a rustic appeal to it. One can find traditional textiles, enormous golf courses, Italian style architecture, and so much more in this town.

Location: Digboi
Best Time To Visit: October to March
Famous for: Housing the oldest refinery in India.
