"Stunning Temples And Sacred Artifacts"


The next on the list of the Assam places to visit is a very ancient pilgrimage site located 24 km northwest of the capital city of Guwahati. Tucked on the banks of Brahmaputra and easily accessible by roads, this is a very distinct and unmissable part of Assam that brings travelers from near and far. The entire area of Hajo is beautifully embellished with stunning temples of Assam and sacred artifacts that you’d love to see when you’re here. Don’t miss the Hayagriva Madhava Mandir and the Hajo Powa Mecca when you’re here. Besides the spiritual places, Hajo also boasts many natural bounties that will you in awe with the site. Thus, Hajo is a must-visit destination for a vacation in Assam. Throughout Monsoon, Hajo is carpeted in verdure, providing a much required visual treat.

Location: Guwahati
Best Time To Visit: October to March
Famous For: Confluence of three major religions – Hindus, Buddhists, Muslims
